About Our Hostels

Boy's Hostel

The boy's hostel was established in 1995 and has a capacity to accommodate 100 students. It is well-equipped with all necessary facilities to support the student's academic and personal needs. The hostel caters to students from both the home district and other districts, offering a conducive environment for study and social interaction. Additionally, a mess facility is available to provide nutritious meals to the residents.

Girl's Hostel

The girl's hostel is a newly constructed facility, built in 2019, and designed to accommodate 50 female students. Like the boy's hostel, it is equipped with all essential amenities to ensure a comfortable and secure stay for the residents. To enhance security, a boundary wall surrounds the girl's hostel, and security personnel are deployed to ensure the safety of the students at all times.
