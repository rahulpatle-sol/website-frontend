MOU with Connect Shiksha +

Connect Shiksha has signed 2nd MOU for 5-year with Government Polytechnic Balaghat to conduct guest lectures in Computer Science, Mechanical, Electrical, and Civil Engineering.

Key Focus Areas:

  1. Industrial Robotics & Automation
  2. Python for Data Science
  3. Electric Vehicle Technology
  4. Embedded Systems & IoT
  5. Drone Design & Mobile Robotics
  6. Hand Robot Design
  7. Web Designing & Development
  8. Data Science & AI
  9. Soft Skills & Career Development
MOU with MSME-TOOL (INDO-GERMAN TOOL ROOM) , INDORE MADHYA Pradesh +

MSME Tool Room (IGTR), Indore will provide technical training programs e.g. AutoCAD, CNC Programming Turning & Milling, STAAD-Pro, PLC, SCADA etc. (software based), vocational trainings, expert lecture, technical visits and adjunct faculties to Government Polytechnic College Balaghat as per their convenience comfort schedule for Diploma students of Mechanical/Electrical/Civil and Computer Science Engineering

Key Focus Areas:

  1. Conducting various training programs for the students of engineering trades to upgrade their skills and knowledge in CNC Technology, CAD/CAM, Architecture Software, PLC, SCADA training, and the analysis field.
  2. Imparting quality education and training to students.
  3. Conducting training and awarding certificates to successful students.
  4. Carrying out similar activities and providing services that increase skills, job opportunities, and productivity on mutual agreement.

MOU with WAINGANGA MINERAL INDUSTRY GARRA BALAGHAT (M.P.) 481001 +

M/s Wainganga Mineral Industry specializes in manufacturing FRP products for mineral benefaction and is establishing a mineral processing plant in Garra Industrial Area (Manganese). It is associated with Hyderabad-based Insmart Systems, a global leader in material handling and quality testing equipment for defense, heavy industry, mining, steel plants, research institutes, and educational institutions. Insmart Systems dominates 95% of India's quality testing market and exports to 32 countries across Asia, Africa, South America, and Europe.

Key Focus Areas:

  1. Joint research in mineral processing and exploration.
  2. Skill development training for students on emerging technologies.
  3. Guest lectures on technology trends and industry requirements.
  4. Faculty training aligned with National Occupational Standards.
  5. Support for student internships and job placements.
  6. Internal approvals, consents, and licenses for program execution.
  7. No financial commitment from Government Polytechnic College Balaghat; separate financial considerations if required.
