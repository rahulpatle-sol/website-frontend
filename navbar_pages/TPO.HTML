About Placement Cell

The Placement Cell of Government Polytechnic College, Balaghat plays a pivotal role in bridging the gap between students and the professional world. It works actively throughout the year to connect students with reputed industries and organizations by organizing campus recruitment drives, internships, and training programs. The cell is committed to enhancing studentsâ€™ employability through skill development initiatives, career counseling, and interview preparation. With a focus on both technical and soft skills, the Placement Cell strives to ensure that students are well-prepared for successful careers in their chosen fields.


The Placement Cell plays a crucial role in locating job opportunities for diploma students passing out from the college by keeping in touch with reputed firms and industrial establishments. The Placement Cell operates round the year to facilitate contacts between companies and graduates.

Training Cell Activities

  • To guide and help in imparting quality practical experience through internship opportunities.
  • To develop confidence building to face the selection process of job recruiters.
  • To provide employment opportunities through campus placements by inviting reputed companies.
  • To provide training for competitive exams like SSC JEE, Banking, Railways, etc.
  • To inform on off-campus job opportunities.
  • To guide in higher education and motivate students for a better career.
  • To promote career counseling by inviting guest speakers and alumni.
  • To prepare students via aptitude tests, GDs, and technical interviews.
