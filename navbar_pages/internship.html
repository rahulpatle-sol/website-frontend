Internship Information

Empowering Students with Industry Exposure

Overview

Internships are essential for academic and professional growth. They offer hands-on exposure, bridge the gap between theory and real-world experience, and boost employability.

Our college encourages internships from the 4th semester onward to prepare students for industry demands.

AICTE Internship Guidelines

AICTE mandates internships for all technical programs to enhance innovation and employability. Key highlights:

  • 4–6 week internships during summer breaks starting 2nd year.
  • Internships accepted in industry, startups, research, NGOs, etc.
  • Colleges must document and evaluate internships for credits.
Download AICTE Guidelines

Internship Process at College

  1. Check eligibility and semester requirements.
  2. Explore opportunities through T&P Cell or external platforms.
  3. Submit internship application form via portals and departments.
  4. Get approval from faculty mentor or department head.
  5. Maintain maximum attendance records
  6. Complete the internship and gather necessary documents.
  7. Submit internship completion certificate and feedback form.
  8. Submit internship report.
