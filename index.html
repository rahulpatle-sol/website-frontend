About GPC Balaghat
AICTE APPROVED & RGPV AFFILIATED
AICTE APPLICATION NO. 1-46478978

Government Polytechnic College Balaghat (MP) was established in July 1962 under Open door policy of Central Government. Initially it was run by a society and became a Government Institute in the year 1984. The area of this college is about 10.50 Acres surrounded by a Wainganga river and a dense teak wood forest. Our college is approved by AICTE and running four diploma courses named Civil, Mechanical, Electrical and Comp. Sc. & Engineering affiliated to Technical University RGPV, Bhopal Madhya Pradesh. The College produces highly skilled technicians which are well placed in reputed companies through campus placement and direct recruitment. Some students go for higher studies. Our college is well equipped with latest instruments having well qualified and experienced faculty and staff members. Our college is committed to produce skilled and expert technicians in various branches of Engineering and also involved in various activities and latest developments for providing value based education to students for their coming bright future.

Message from Principal

"Empowering minds, inspiring innovation, and shaping the leaders of tomorrow"

Welcome to Government Polytechnic colllege Balaghat. As the principal, it is my privilege to lead an institution that stands at the forefront of educational innovation and academic distinction.

Our college is more than just a place of learning; it's a vibrant community where ideas flourish, talents are nurtured, and futures are shaped. We pride ourselves on our cutting-edge facilities, distinguished faculty, and a curriculum that seamlessly blends traditional wisdom with modern technological advancements.

At Government Polytechnic College Balaghat, we believe in fostering an environment that encourages critical thinking, creativity, and personal growth. Our goal is not just to impart knowledge, but to inspire a lifelong love for learning and a commitment to excellence in all endeavors.

We are dedicated to preparing our students for the challenges of the rapidly evolving global landscape. Through innovative teaching methodologies, industry partnerships, and a focus on holistic development, we ensure that our graduates are not just job-ready, but are capable of becoming leaders and change-makers in their chosen fields.
