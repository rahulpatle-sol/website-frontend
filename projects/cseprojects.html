E-Panchayat Garhi

e-Panchayat Garhi is a digital initiative aimed at empowering the rural community of Garhi village through technology-driven governance and development. This platform serves as a centralized digital hub for the villagers, providing transparent access to information, government schemes, village development plans, notices, services, and local administration updates.

Website Of Government Polytechnic College

With the growing importance of digital presence of “College Website” this project seeks to our college Government Polytechnic College Balaghat, this website is affiliated by Government Polytechnic College Balaghat Which Provides diploma courses that help students gain practical skills and useful knowledge for real jobs, Our goal is to help students build a strong future by preparing them for careers and further studies The main purpose of this website is to provide information to all of those students those are being studying or interested to get here admission by providing them information such as Notices, News, Academic results, and Admission Procedure, Our College website includes an overview of our college tour on our homepage which is done by aerial videography through Drone camera, which can be easier for the students to get to know about the college campus without visiting the college. The website has some beneficial features such as overview of the college Department details, Faculty details, Academic calendar, Latest news, Notifications, Results, Scholarships information and contact information. Apart from this website also has an admin login system and admin dashboard where the admin can update and manage the information this website has been built using modern web technology such as HTML, CSS, & JAVASCRIPT for the frontend and PHP & MYSQL database for the backend for providing security, fast and easier maintenance, This website has been made full responsive so every user can render well on a variety of devices and window or screen sizes from minimum to maximum display size to ensure usability and satisfaction.

E-Garden Mulna Vatika

Our project is a web-based portal developed to showcase the garden of our college, providing detailed information about various plants present on the campus along with their botanical details and practical uses. The primary objective is to promote environmental awareness, support educational purposes, and create a digital archive of the flora within the institution.

Hardware Maintenance and Troubleshooting Website

Our project focuses on hardware maintenance, where we explored and understood the internal components of a computer system, including the CPU, RAM, motherboard, hard drives, power supply, and peripheral devices. Through hands-on learning and troubleshooting exercises, we gained practical knowledge of identifying, diagnosing, and resolving common hardware issues. To extend the utility of our learning, we developed a user-friendly website that serves as a resource hub for hardware-related problems and their solutions. The website categorizes common hardware issues and provides step-by-step guides, tips, and troubleshooting techniques. It is designed to assist users—from beginners to tech enthusiasts—in maintaining their systems effectively. This project not only enhanced our technical skills in hardware but also integrated web development practices to share our knowledge with a wider audience.
