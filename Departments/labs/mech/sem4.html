Semester 4 Lab Guide

Measurements and Methodology Lab

Welcome to the Fluid Mechanics and Hydraulic Machinery Lab!:

  • Measure the diameter of a wire using micrometre and compare the result with a digital micrometre.
  • Measure the angle of the machined surface using a sine bar with slip gauges.
  • Measure the angle of a V-block / Taper Shank of Drill / Dovetail using a universal bevel protractor.
  • Measure the dimensions of ground MS flat/cylindrical bush using a Vernier Caliper and compare with a Digital/Dial Vernier Caliper.
  • Measure the geometrical dimensions of a V-Thread using a thread Vernier gauge.
  • Measure the thickness of ground MS plates using slip gauges.

Strength of Materials

  • Prepare a specimen and examine the microstructure of the Ferrous and Non- ferrous met- als using the Metallurgical Microscope.
  • Detect the cracks in the specimen using (i) Visual inspection and ring test (ii) Die penetra- tion test (iii) Magnetic particle test.
  • Determination of Rockwell’s Hardness Number for various materials like mild steel, high carbon steel, brass, copper and aluminium.
  • Finding the resistance of materials to impact loads by Izod test and Charpy test.
  • Torsion test on mild steel – relation between torque and angle of twist determination of shear modulus and shear stress.
  • Finding Young’s Modulus of Elasticity, yield points, percentage elongation and percentage reduction in area, stress strain diagram plotting, tests on mild steel.
  • Determination of modulus of rigidity, strain energy, shear stress and stiffness by load de- flection method (Open & Closed coil spring)
  • Single or double Shear test on M.S. bar to finding the resistance of material to shear load.

Thermal Engineering-II

  • Study of high pressure boiler with model.
  • Study of boiler mountings and accessories.
  • Conduct performance test on VCR test rig to determine COP of the refrigerator.
  • Conduct performance test on multi-stage reciprocating compressor.
  • Conduct Morse test to determine the indicated power of individual cylinders.
  • Conduct performance test on 2-S CI/SI engine.
  • Conduct performance test on 4-S CI/SI engine.
  • Conduct heat balance test on CI/SI engine.
  • Conduct economical speed test on 4-S CI/SI engine.
  • Perform thermal conductivity test on:
    • Thick slab
    • Composite wall
    • Thick cylinder
  • Leak detection of refrigeration equipment.
  • Conduct performance test on A/C test rig to determine COP of the refrigerator.
