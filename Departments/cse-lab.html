About Our Lab

Our state-of-the-art computer lab provides students with access to the latest hardware and software. Equipped with high-speed internet, modern desktops, and advanced tools, it supports a wide range of computing needs from basic coursework to advanced research projects.

The lab is designed to foster collaboration, innovation, and technical skill development in a comfortable and productive environment. Our dedicated staff is always available to provide assistance and ensure all equipment is functioning optimally.

Facilities

50+ High-Performance Computers

Latest generation processors, ample RAM, and SSD storage for fast performance

High-Speed Wi-Fi

Gigabit internet connection throughout the facility for seamless online work

Projectors & Smart Boards

Interactive presentation equipment for collaborative learning and teaching

Printing & Scanning Services

Color and black & white printing with high-resolution scanning capabilities
