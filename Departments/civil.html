About Department

The CIVIL Engineering Department is one of the oldest and most challenging branch of Engineering. The Diploma course at Govt. Polytechnic college Balaghat is equipped to meet the present day technological and industrial requirements . The department emphasizes more on practical applications of engineering concepts in order to make students skilled professionals. The department is committed to well being and all round development of its students.


The objective of the CIVIL Engineering Diploma program is to prepare the manpower that is required in industries/ MNCS / public services . Most of the students , who graduate from the department, end up taking good job in industry / MNC / public services.

Our Mission

The mission of the Civil Engineering Department at Government Polytechnic College Balaghat is to deliver high-quality technical education and practical training that prepares students for real-world challenges in the field of civil engineering. We are committed to developing knowledgeable, skilled, and ethical diploma engineers who are capable of contributing to the planning, design, and execution of sustainable infrastructure projects. Our department encourages innovation, teamwork, and continuous learning to help students grow both professionally and personally.

Our Vision

To be a premier department in civil engineering education that inspires excellence, fosters creativity, and supports sustainable development. The Civil Engineering Department at Government Polytechnic College Balaghat envisions shaping future-ready professionals who can contribute effectively to society through advanced technical skills, environmental responsibility, and leadership in the construction industry. We aim to create a learning environment that supports research, collaboration, and a passion for building a better world.

PO's, PEO's & PSO's

PO'S

  • Basic knowledge: An ability to apply knowledge of basic mathematics, science and engineering to solve the engineering problems.
  • Discipline knowledge: An ability to apply discipline-specific knowledge to solve core and/or Applied engineering problems.
  • Experiments and practice: An ability to plan and perform experiments and practices and to use the results to solve engineering problems.
  • Engineering Tools: Apply appropriate technologies and tools with an understanding of the Limitations.

PSO'S

  • Supervise civil engineering constructions and maintenance work applying appropriate construction techniques.
  • Understand modern management and construction techniques to complete the project within the stipulated time and funds.
  • Implement prevailing codal provisions, environmental and safety regulations during the execution of civil engineering projects.
