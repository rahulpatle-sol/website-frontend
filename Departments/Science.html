About Department

The Science and Humanities Engineering Department at GPC Balaghat is dedicated to providing cutting-edge education and research opportunities in the field of civil engineering. Our programs prepare students for exciting careers in infrastructure development and innovation. The Civil Engineering Department is one of the evolving departments of the institute which was established in the year 2007. The department has well-equipped laboratories where students are well trained under the guidance of experienced faculty. Civil engineering is essential for all infrastructure development due to which the career possibilities are endless. The present intake capacity of the department is 60, having qualified faculty members and supporting staff. The higher-ranked candidates in PPT opt for admission in Diploma (Civil Engineering) at this department. Students passing out from this department get good placements. Since civil engineering skills are integral to modern infrastructure development, you're likely to find our civil engineering skills in high demand across many different industries.

Message from HOD

"Humanity without science is fragile; science without humanity is lethal."

The Science and Humanities Department at Government Polytechnic College, Balaghat forms the academic foundation for all diploma engineering programs offered at the institution. With a mission to cultivate scientific temper, critical thinking, and strong communication skills among students, the department plays a pivotal role in nurturing well-rounded technical professionals.

Our curriculum covers key subjects such as Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, English, Environmental Studies, and Professional Communication, ensuring students develop a comprehensive understanding of the basic sciences and human values that are essential for engineering excellence.

The department boasts highly qualified and experienced faculty members who are committed to delivering quality education through innovative teaching methodologies, laboratory experiments, and project-based learning. By fostering a spirit of inquiry and ethical responsibility, the Science and Humanities Department equips students with the analytical abilities, creativity, and leadership qualities needed to thrive in the modern technological world.
