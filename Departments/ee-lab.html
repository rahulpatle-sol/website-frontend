About Our Labs

Electrical Lab: The Electrical Lab is a well-equipped space designed to provide students with hands-on experience in electrical engineering concepts. It allows practical exploration of circuits, wiring, transformers, motors, and electrical measurements. Through guided experiments and real-world simulations, students gain valuable skills in analyzing and troubleshooting electrical systems, preparing them for both academic and industry challenges.
