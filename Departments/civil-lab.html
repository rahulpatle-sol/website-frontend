About Our Labs

Soil Testing and Mechanics Lab: The Soil Testing and Mechanics Lab provides a practical learning environment for understanding soil behavior and characteristics. It supports geotechnical studies by allowing students to perform various tests related to soil strength, permeability, and compaction. This lab plays a vital role in preparing students for real-world applications in civil and construction engineering.
Facilities – Soil Testing and Mechanics Lab:

  1. Standard Proctor compaction test apparatus
  2. Permeability testing setup
  3. Direct shear and triaxial test machines
  4. Sieve analysis and hydrometer testing equipment
